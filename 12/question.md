**fizzBuzz**

Write a function that prints numbers from 1 to n. For multiples of 3, print "Fizz" instead of the number. For multiples of 5, print "Buzz". For multiples of both, print "FizzBuzz".

```js
example;
input: 15;
output: 1;
2;
Fizz;
4;
Buzz;
Fizz;
7;
8;
Fizz;
Buzz;
11;
Fizz;
13;
14;
FizzBuzz;
```
